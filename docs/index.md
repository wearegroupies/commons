# Groupies Commons

Groupies Commons is the open, structured archive of live music events in the Netherlands — built to make concert history findable, verifiable, and useful.

The archive spans from 1953 to the present, covering artists, venues, and events across the country. Records are held to a canonical schema and validated against primary sources wherever possible.

Start with the [Charter](charter.md) to understand the principles behind the project, or go straight to the [Schema](schema/index.md) to see how data is structured.
